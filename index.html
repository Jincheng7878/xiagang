Who We Are

Engineering the Future of Power

Jiangsu Xiagang Electric Power Co., Ltd. was founded in 2018 and is headquartered in Wuxi, Jiangsu – a major industrial hub in China. We are a Grade II licensed contractor in construction, power engineering, and communication engineering, providing integrated EPC services for power infrastructure projects ranging from 10kV to 220kV.

Our core business includes substation construction, transmission & distribution lines, equipment supply, renewable energy integration, and O&M services. We serve state-owned grid operators, telecom giants (China Mobile, China Telecom, China Unicom), data centers, industrial parks, and government agencies.

With a registered capital of RMB 35.88 million and over 7,263 m² of office and warehouse space (plus a 4,000+ m² production base in Yixing), we have completed 150+ successful projects across Jiangsu, Hebei, and other provinces. Our annual revenue exceeds hundreds of millions RMB.

We are proud to hold Grade II General Contracting for Construction, Power, and Communication Engineering, a Power Facility License (Installation Class III / Repair Class II / Testing Class II), ISO 9001/14001/45001 certifications, 9 utility patents, and 11+ AAA credit ratings. Our team of 165+ professionals includes 15 project managers, 25 engineers, and 60+ specialized technicians.

As a strategic partner of State Grid Jiangsu, China Mobile, China Tower, and other leading enterprises, we are committed to delivering reliable, innovative, and sustainable power solutions – both in China and for global clients.
