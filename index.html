Who We Are

Engineering the Future of Power

Founded in 2018 in Wuxi, Jiangsu, China, Xiagang Electric Power integrates R&D, design, manufacturing, sales, and construction. We provide high-reliability power engineering solutions for state grid, telecom operators, industrial parks, and renewable energy developers.

With RMB 35.88 million registered capital and 7,263 m² of office and warehouse, our annual revenue exceeds hundreds of millions. We hold Grade II General Contracting for Construction, Grade III for Power Engineering, National Energy Administration license, ISO certifications, and 18+ patents.

⚡ Core Expertise: 10kV distribution · 35kV/110kV/220kV substations · transmission lines · equipment supply · O&M.
