OUR STORY

Engineering the Future of Power Since 2018

Reliability · Innovation · Sustainability

Headquartered in Wuxi, Jiangsu, Xiagang Electric Power is a full-service power engineering company serving State Grid, industrial parks, data centers, and global clients.

Who We Are

Company Profile

Founded on August 21, 2018, with a registered capital of 35.88 million RMB, Xiagang Electric Power is located in Wuxi, Jiangsu Province. We own a 3,350 m² modern office building and a 3,913 m² fixed warehouse, plus a production base of 4,000+ m² in Yixing. Our annual revenue exceeds hundreds of millions RMB.

We specialize in power distribution, transmission, and substation EPC, covering 10kV distribution and 35kV, 110kV, 220kV transmission & transformation projects – from design and equipment supply to construction and commissioning. We serve major communication operators, equipment manufacturers, State Grid, government agencies, and industrial enterprises.

Background of Establishment

The company understands that electricity is the cornerstone of modern society, and efficient equipment and high-quality engineering are essential.
